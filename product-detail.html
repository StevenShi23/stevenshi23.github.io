Concept Details

This collection imagines clothing as part of a dreamy game-like interface, where fashion becomes a way to signal softness, personality, and quiet fantasy. The visual language is gentle, playful, and emotionally comforting.

Brand Direction

As a concept project, Dream Save represents how The Moon Patch brings together fashion styling, mood design, and gaming-inspired storytelling into one soft visual world.
