The Moon Patch FAQs

The Moon Patch is a fashion x gaming concept brand that blends soft visual storytelling, fantasy-inspired styling, and cozy emotional design. It imagines fashion as a playful world where identity, mood, and imagination come together.

Both fashion and games help people build identity. In fashion, people express themselves through clothing, textures, and styling. In games, players shape characters, moods, and worlds. The Moon Patch connects these two creative spaces through customization, imagination, and comfort.

The brand follows a soft, dreamy, and playful aesthetic. It draws from moonlit palettes, cozy fantasy games, cute interfaces, gentle textures, and emotionally warm visual worlds.

Brand Info

Right now, The Moon Patch is a concept website created for a class project. It presents a brand identity, collection ideas, and a fashion world inspired by gaming aesthetics and storytelling.
